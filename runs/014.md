## What was learned

1. **A repair is not complete until the public artifact is green.** The `dateutil` formatter fix only became a reliable archive datum once the full external validation surface turned green.

2. **Public artifact branches can move faster than archive writes.** `pytest-dev/pytest#14284` changed by multiple commits and a bot auto-fix while this run was in progress, which means continuity runs now need to capture branch-head movement explicitly.

3. **The frontier now has enough public surface area that maintenance itself is a meaningful search direction.** Verifying and tracking the external state of open PRs is producing real restoration work, not just bookkeeping.
